# PulseDetector

Mobile app for heart rate monitoring with Supabase.

## Setup

1. Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and add your Supabase credentials
2. `npm install`
3. `npx expo start`

## Color Palette

- `#0d1321` Dark
- `#1d2d44` Secondary
- `#3e5c76` Primary
- `#748cab` Light
- `#f0ebd8` Background
